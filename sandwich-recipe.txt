Ingredients:
 - 4 slices of bread
 - 10gms sandwich spread
 - 1 tablespoon capsicum, finely chopped
 - 2 tablespoon cucumber, finely chopped
 - 2 tablespoon tomato, finely chopped
 - 1 tablespoon boiled american corn
 - 1 teaspoon black pepper
 
Directions:
 1. Mix capsicum, cucumber, tomato, american corn and black pepper in a bowl.
 2. Take bread slices and apply sandwich spread on one side of each slice.
 3. Take a slice with sandwich spread on top and place a table spoon of the 
	mixture on it.
 4. Cover it with another slice with sandwich spread facing down.
 5. Repeat step 3 and 4 for another pair of slices.
 6. Grill the sandwich for 5 minutes if you want or have it just like that.
 7. Serve with tomato ketchup.
